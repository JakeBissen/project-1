Why Our Hay?

Now, you might be thinking to yourself, why our hay? Well, our hay is grown with care and attention to detail. We don't drown our hay in pesticides, and we don't hay-te our hay, we appricate it! Our hay is grown in fertile fields roughly 10 acres from roads and people, and we make sure to remove pest like Johnson grass before bailing our hay! We hand stack and store each bale to insure quality, as well as mostiure test each one for maxium quality ensurance.
